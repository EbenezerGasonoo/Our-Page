About Us

Why we are awesome

We are passionate Gamers,Geeks,Nerds who love to report gameing news and more to the Ghanaian public and bring a new light on gamification that is on various game studios and communities in Ghana and in Africa as a whole.

Whilst we started small we have always wanted to push and game news locally first and the internationally,

Our Goal is to push our country's negative image of video games and let a of people realise there are careers to have from video games.

We want to push in our own country game devs,game events,and all that is gaming related in in this peaceful country we call home.Come join us on this epic journey.
